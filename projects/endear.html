Overview

Trail Angel is a mobile app that assists active people to plan adventures outdoors without worrying about air pollution due to wildfires. The app includes a connection to a portable air tracker to monitor conditions in real-time. Trail Angel also provides alternative route planning should an area become unsafe, so outdoor activities can still be enjoyed safely.

Solution

On-time Information

Individuals can employ the portable air quality monitor to instantaneously visualize and continuously monitor real-time air quality.

Make a Trail Plan

Users can access a sophisticated platform that not only facilitates the creation of personalized outdoor trail plans but also provides real-time air quality index data. This empowers users to enjoy a pristine outdoor experience, ensuring they breathe in clean, fresh air throughout their journey.

Detour A Trail

Users can enhance awarenesses of real-time outdoor local air pollution alerts, enabling them to promptly assess the safety of their current location with respect to air quality.

Change A Trail Plan

Users can access real-time air pollution forecasts, allowing them to adjust their travel plans proactively prior to reaching their destination.

Users seek an app for outdoor activity planning, especially hiking and camping, that integrates trail information with real-time air quality data, especially during wildfire seasons.

Wildfire Impact & Air Quality Solutions Summary:

Wildfires hurt people's health and their feelings. People are scared and upset because they can't control wildfires.

People need to know where the smoke from wildfires is going and if it's safe to go outside. They want this information quickly and in a way they can trust.

Predictable Information:

People want to know what to expect with air quality and wildfires so they can plan ahead.

Real-Time Accuracy:

The information given to people about wildfires and air quality must be right and up to date. They are frustrated with wrong information.

Special Needs for Certain People:

Some people, like those with allergies, need more detailed information that is specific to their health needs.

Conclusion:

Competitors

AllTrails Marketing Analyse

AllTrails is a comprehensive route guide by region, designed for those who do not run in the city simply because they don't know there are trails nearby. The app allows users to search by area to find the nearest trails and find information about those trails.

Pros

  • Trail Search
  • Filtering options
  • Ease of use

Cons

  • Offline map for Pro members only
  • Lack of detailed information

SWOT/PEST Analysis

Strength

Provides distances and user ratings, reviews, and user recordings crowdsourced for each trail, as well as photos uploaded by millions of registered hikers, mountain bikers, and trail runners. Alltrails has over 50,000 trails with details. Its datalg helps people explore the outdoors with trail maps.

Weakness

The E-book map does not offer interactive location searches.

Opportunities

  • P: Some places still require masks because of COVID.
  • E: AllTrails raised $150M after COVID.
  • S: Global Community.
  • T: GPS navigation.

Threats

  • P: COVID lockdowns (for now) have largely ended.
  • E: Well-known in hiking marketing, member cost risk.
  • S: The app is more utilitarian than it is social.
  • T: Organic keywords more related to hiking.

BreezoMeter Marketing Analyse

BreezoMeter combines big data and machine learning technology to provide intuitive, personalised information on air quality and pollen levels to companies and consumers around the world. It scales business with BreezoMeter's environmental intelligence platform & API products. Provides Air Quality API for accuracy-Monitored, real Time & forecast, etc.

Pros

  • The app is easy to use.
  • Great for adding air quality maps and data to our product.

Cons

Takeaways

What I Learned

Segmentation:

Choosing the right market segments is key. Good segmentation helps in making better products and marketing them.

Learning from Competitors:

Look at what competitors do and don't do well to find chances to do better.

User Insight:

Know why and how users act, including psychological and biological reasons, to make products they'll love.

Methodology Fit:

Use methods that work well for your project to get reliable results.

Brand Strategy:

Have a clear brand strategy to show what your brand stands for and what makes it different.

Brand in Design:

Make sure your brand's style shows up in your UX/UI to make a lasting impression and keep users coming back.

Practical Tech Use:

Be smart about using technology to keep costs down and make sure your project works well.

Next Step

Review the current project plan to ensure it aligns with our key learnings. This includes checking our market segmentation, evaluating our competitors for any opportunities, deepening our understanding of user behavior, ensuring our methodology is project-specific, refining our brand strategy, integrating our brand identity into UX/UI design, and planning a realistic technology implementation strategy. Set a meeting with the team to discuss these points and make necessary adjustments to the project plan.
