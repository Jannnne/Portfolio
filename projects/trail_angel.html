Overview

Trail Angel is a mobile app that assists active people to plan adventures outdoors without worrying about air pollution due to wildfires. The app includes a connection to a portable air tracker to monitor conditions in real-time. Trail Angel also provides alternative route planning should an area become unsafe, so outdoor activities can still be enjoyed safely.

Solution

On-time Information

Individuals can employ the portable air quality monitor to instantaneously visualize and continuously monitor real-time air quality.

Make a Trail Plan

Users can access a sophisticated platform that not only facilitates the creation of personalized outdoor trail plans but also provides real-time air quality index data. This empowers users to enjoy a pristine outdoor experience, ensuring they breathe in clean, fresh air throughout their journey.

Detour A Trail

Users can enhance awarenesses of real-time outdoor local air pollution alerts, enabling them to promptly assess the safety of their current location with respect to air quality.

Change A Trail Plan

Users can access real-time air pollution forecasts, allowing them to adjust their travel plans proactively prior to reaching their destination.

Health Impact

For some wildfire smoke, PM2.5 is used to make health recommendations : It defines the cutoff for particles that can travel deep into the lungs and cause the most damage. According to a report released by the American Lung Association, wildfires were responsible for the rise in pollution in the western U.S. 2.1 million more Americans now live in counties with unhealthy air.

Interview

Audiences Interview

Users want to have more predictable and accurate information, alerts, and AQI tracking reports. Users need an app that is practical and can tell where the emergency and supply facilities are. Household families might already know about the air quality because of media, neighbors, and friends.

Experts Interview

Target audience segmentation is essential to solve the problem. Make one concept really clear to benefit the audience. Designers need to know users’ behavior patterns. It will mean that the air monitor has some kind of GPS system, and also a GPS module and audio. It can connect to the internet.

Affinity Diagram

Wildfires do not only have health physical impacts on humans, they also have an emotional effect. What my target audience needs: Valuable air quality Information, wildfire data Accurate wildfire, and Air Quality real-time predictions and alerts.

Antithesis

Project Goals

Business Goals

  • Sell more than 100 air track monitors in one week with our new Trail Angel software.
  • Partner with BreezoMeter and use its Hyper-local Environmental Intelligence, Wildfire Tracker +, and Historical Environmental Data.

Product Goals

  • Have users sign up.
  • Allow users using Trail Angel to make trail plans.
  • Allow users to access wildfire history maps to check trail conditions.
  • Allow users to have real-time alerts and access pollution details.
  • Allow users to change trail plans after getting alerts.
  • Allow users to connect with an air tracker monitoring to get a location warning.

Users & Their Goals

  • Nature enthusiasts who are into the outdoors in the San Francisco Bay Area.
  • Bay Area outdoor hikers, cyclists, and joggers.
  • People who want to be active outdoors during wildfire season.

The Risk

  • Users do not want to buy an air tracker monitor.
  • Users still stick to the apps they are used to.
  • Cell Phones and monitors might run out of power.

Market Size

The market size is overlap with following segmentation:

  • Weather closely followers
  • Outdoor Activity People
  • Healthy Sensitive group
